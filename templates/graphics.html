Introduction

flipnotes.net is a web-based application for taking both private note and public notes. perfect for simple and clean presentation of your ideas.

In today's electronic world, it can be quite a challenge to organize all useful information such as web clips, articles, bits of text, images, interesting web links, bookmarks, shopping lists, to-do lists, recipes, tips, passwords, secretkeys, contact list, everyday task, ideas and many others that are hanging around US. Simplenote is an excellent solution to manage these snippets of information.
